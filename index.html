This was my school app project

This is a gamified productivity app. It is called "Life of Mr. Useless". It is an app that motivates people to use the phone less. The player will be rewarded with in-game leveling up and unlocking more items and achievements, if they turn on the game and promise to do something (like read a book, go running, meditate and so on). They can also link it to social media and compete with friends.

I was in charge of the graphic, animation, user Interface and the game flow.
